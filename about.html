About Aurora

This website is inspired by the Northern Lights — a natural light display seen in Arctic regions. The smooth gradients and motion reflect calm, silence, and cosmic beauty.

Northern lights, or auroras, occur when charged particles from the sun interact with Earth's magnetic field. This phenomenon creates stunning light displays.
